Avocado Farming for Profit

Learn how to grow, harvest, and profit from avocados

🪴 Best Season to Plant

Plant avocados during late winter to early spring. The ideal temperature is between 15°C to 30°C. Ensure the frost has passed.

🌱 How to Farm Avocados

  • Choose well-drained soil, preferably sandy loam.
  • Ensure full sunlight exposure for 6–8 hours daily.
  • Space trees 20–30 feet apart.
  • Water regularly, but avoid waterlogging.
  • Use organic mulch and compost for best growth.

💰 Ideas for Getting Profit

  • Sell directly to local markets and organic stores.
  • Start an avocado-based product line (oil, cosmetics, spreads).
  • Export to countries with high demand (e.g., Europe, Middle East).
  • Promote on social media as “farm fresh & organic.”
  • Collaborate with restaurants for regular supply contracts.

📦 Additional Tips

Prune trees regularly, protect from pests like mites and root rot, and harvest when the fruit turns dark green but still firm. You can earn up to ₹5–10 lakh per acre annually with proper care and market strategy.
